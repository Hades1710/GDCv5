How It Works

Understand our donation process and how we help those in need

For Donors: How to Make a Difference

1

Create Your Donor Account

Sign up as a donor by providing your basic information and creating your account. This helps us verify your identity and keep track of your generous contributions.

During registration, you'll also set your donation preferences to help match you with students who align with your giving goals:

  • Student backgrounds you wish to support
  • Educational interests you want to encourage
  • Specific needs you'd like to address
2

Browse Student Requests

Once registered, you'll have access to your personalized dashboard that shows student requests matching your preferences. Each request includes:

  • The student's profile and background
  • Reason for requesting assistance
  • Amount requested
  • Current progress toward their goal

Click on any request card to see additional details like educational interests, specific needs, and more information about the student.

3

Make Your Donation

When you find a request that resonates with you, simply click "Proceed to Payment" on the request card. On the payment page, you can:

  • Enter the amount you wish to donate (within your balance)
  • Review the student's details one more time
  • Complete the payment process

Our secure payment system will process your donation immediately, and the amount will be deducted from your account balance.

4

Track Your Impact

After completing your donation, you'll receive a confirmation with transaction details. You can always view your donation history in your dashboard.

Watch as the student's funding progress bar increases with your contribution. You can track:

  • Your total contributions
  • How many students you've helped
  • The progress of requests you've contributed to

For Students: How to Request Support

1

Create Your Student Account

Sign up as a student by providing your personal information and academic details. This helps us verify your eligibility and match you with potential donors.

Your profile should include:

  • Your basic contact information
  • Current educational status
  • A profile photo (optional but recommended)
2

Submit Your Request

From your student dashboard, click on "Make Request" to submit a new funding request. You'll need to provide:

  • Your name and age
  • Reason for requesting financial assistance
  • Current education level
  • Annual family income
  • Educational interests
  • Specific needs you have
  • The amount you're requesting

Be specific and honest about your situation to help donors understand how they can make a difference in your education.

3

Request Review Process

Once submitted, your request will be reviewed by our team to ensure it meets our eligibility criteria:

  • Verified student status
  • Clear educational purpose
  • Reasonable request amount
  • Complete and accurate information

Approved requests will be visible to potential donors who match your educational interests and specific needs.

4

Receive Your Funds

As donors contribute to your request, you'll see the progress in your dashboard. Once your request is fully funded:

  • You'll receive notification of completed funding
  • Our team will process the disbursement
  • Funds will be provided according to the specific needs outlined in your request

We may require documentation for certain educational expenses to ensure the funds are used as intended.
